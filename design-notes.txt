Reference Site Design Notes - Drills Page

HEADER:
- "Training Library" title in italic/script font
- Subtitle: "Browse our curated collection of professional drills designed to elevate every aspect of your game."

SEARCH BAR:
- Full width search input
- Placeholder: "Search for drills..."
- Search icon on left

DIFFICULTY FILTER:
- Label: "Difficulty"
- Pill buttons in a row: All, Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced
- "All" is selected by default (highlighted)

CATEGORY FILTER:
- Label: "Category"
- Pill buttons: All Categories (cyan highlighted), Mechanics, Hand-Eye Coordination, Game Situations, Bunting, Power, Pitch Recognition

RESULTS COUNT:
- "Found 24 drills" text

DRILL CARDS:
- 3 column grid
- Featured image at top (full width of card)
- Difficulty badge in top-right corner of image (Beginner=green, Intermediate=yellow, Advanced=red)
- Category tag below image (cyan dot + uppercase text like "MECHANICS", "TEE WORK")
- Drill title in bold white
- Brief description in gray text
- "View Details" link at bottom

CARD STYLING:
- Dark background
- Rounded corners
- Hover effects
